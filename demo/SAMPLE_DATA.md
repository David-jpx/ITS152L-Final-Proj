## Key Features Demonstrated

- **Asset Status Tracking**: Assets can be "Active", "Needs Repair", "Approved", "Rejected"
- **Maintenance Scheduling**: Assets due for maintenance (6+ months) are highlighted
- **Expense Tracking**: Each repair/maintenance activity is recorded as an expense
- **Budget Management**: Expenses are tracked against departmental budgets
- **Asset Categories**: Different types of IT equipment (Laptops, Routers, Switches, Cables)
